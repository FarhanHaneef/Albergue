About Albergue

Albergue is a comprehensive solution designed to revolutionize hostel operations.

From seamless student registration to efficient attendance tracking, billing management, and QR-based leave application, the system addresses the challenges of traditional hostel management with innovative technology.

By integrating features such as a complaint and feedback system, real-time notifications, and an intuitive admin dashboard, Albergue fosters transparency, improves communication, and enhances the overall hostel experience for students and administrators alike.

Built with a robust and scalable technology stack including HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Firebase, the system is adaptable to future enhancements, ensuring it remains relevant and efficient as needs evolve.
